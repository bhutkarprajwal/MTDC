Top Tourist Places in Maharashtra

Explore some of the most popular and scenic destinations in Maharashtra.

Ajanta Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its ancient rock-cut caves and beautiful murals.

Lonavala

A picturesque hill station known for its beautiful valleys, waterfalls, and lush greenery.

Mahabaleshwar

Famous hill station offering panoramic views, waterfalls, and beautiful lakes.

Elephanata cave

Ancient cave temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on Elephanta Island near Mumbai.

Chikhaldara

Hindus claim that this place was featured in the epic of the Mahabharata.

Harihar fort

The Harihar fort is a triangular rock formation. From the base village, it looks like a rectangular shape.

Festivals

Maharashtra is a land of vibrant festivals that reflect its rich cultural heritage and traditions. From the grand celebrations of Ganesh Chaturthi, where streets come alive with colorful processions and devotional songs, to the joyous festivities of Gudi Padwa, marking the Maharashtrian New Year, each festival holds deep cultural significance. Festivals like Makar Sankranti, Nag Panchami, and Diwali are celebrated with equal enthusiasm, bringing families and communities together. These celebrations offer a glimpse into Maharashtra's rich history, spirituality, and artistic expression, making them an integral part of the state's cultural identity
